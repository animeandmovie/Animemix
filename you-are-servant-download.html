Synopsis: "You are Servant" tells the story of Kael, an ordinary young man whose life takes a dramatic turn when he discovers he is bound by an unbreakable, centuries-old magical pact. This pact forces him into the service of Elara, a powerful and enigmatic sorceress from a hidden realm, who appears with little warning and even less explanation.

Thrust into a world of arcane magic, treacherous political games between mystical factions, and dangerous quests to retrieve ancient artifacts, Kael must quickly adapt to his new role. Elara is a demanding master, her motives often shrouded in mystery, yet she occasionally shows glimpses of a forgotten past that ties her fate to Kael's. As Kael hones his own latent abilities under Elara's tutelage (and her often harsh commands), he begins to unravel the truth behind the pact, the reasons for Elara's mission in his world, and the powerful enemies who seek to exploit their bond. Is he merely a tool, or does his servitude hold the key to a destiny far greater than he could have ever imagined?
