Synopsis: "Miss Hammurabi" tells the story of Park Cha Oh-reum, a newly appointed, passionate, and idealistic judge in her mid-20s who works at the Seoul Central District Court. The drama follows these three judges with very different personalities and perspectives as they tackle a variety of realistic civil cases, from sexual harassment and domestic violence to corporate power abuse, offering a critical look at Korean society and the challenges within its legal framework.
